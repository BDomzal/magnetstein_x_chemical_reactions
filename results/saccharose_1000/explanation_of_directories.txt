Explanation of what is kept in directories:

1) cut_spectra_of_components_from_mixture_cut_spectra_of_mixture
-> Results for input: data/saccharose1000/preprocessed_mixture.csv, data/saccharose1000/preprocessed_saccharose_from_mixture.csv, data/saccharose1000/preprocessed_glucose_from_mixture.csv, data/saccharose1000/preprocessed_fructose_from_mixture.csv
Additionally, the baseline was corrected in mixture! (See notebooks/preprocessing/Saccharose_hydrolysis_preprocessing.ipynb).

2) cut_spectra_of_components_from_mixture_full_spectra_of_mixture
-> Results for input: data/saccharose1000/all.csv, data/saccharose1000/preprocessed_saccharose_from_mixture.csv, data/saccharose1000/preprocessed_glucose_from_mixture.csv, data/saccharose1000/preprocessed_fructose_from_mixture.csv

3) cut_spectra_of_components_from_separate_files_full_spectra_of_mixture
-> Results for input: data/saccharose1000/all.csv, data/saccharose1000/preprocessed_saccharose.csv, data/saccharose1000/preprocessed_glucose.csv, data/saccharose1000/preprocessed_fructose.csv

4) full_spectra_of_components_from_separate_files_full_spectra_of_mixture
-> Results for input: data/saccharose1000/all.csv, data/saccharose1000/sucrose.csv, data/saccharose1000/glucose.csv, data/saccharose1000/fructose.csv
